Kontomire Stew Recipe

Kontomire Stew is one of the nutritional dishes of Ghanaians. It is very healthy and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Momone
  • Palm Oil
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Smoked Fish
  • Akatewa
  • Meat
  • Spices
  • Kontomire Leaves

Steps In preparing

  1. Pour your palm oil into your pan
  2. Fry the palm oil for a while and add your chopped onions
  3. Put in your Momone and continue to stir
  4. Pour in your blended pepper,onions and tomatoes and continue to stir
  5. Sprinkle your spices and and break the smoked fish to your preferred sizes
  6. Pour in your chopped steamed meat
  7. Cover and wait for five minutes and continue stirring
  8. Put in a tablespoon of salt and maggi for taste
  9. Your stew should be ready in 10 minutes
