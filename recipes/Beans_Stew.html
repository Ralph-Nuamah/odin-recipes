Beans Stew Recipe

Beans Stew is one of the nutritional dishes of Ghanaians. It is very healthy and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Momone
  • Palm Oil
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Smoked Fish
  • Akatewa
  • Meat
  • Spices
  • Beans

Steps In preparing

  1. Boil your beans till ready. Mostly between 1 hour to 2 hours
  2. Pour your palm oil into your pan
  3. Fry the palm oil for a while and add your chopped onions
  4. Put in your Momone and continue to stir
  5. Pour in your blended pepper,onions and tomatoes and continue to stir
  6. Sprinkle your spices and and break the smoked fish to your preferred sizes
  7. Pour in your chopped steamed meat
  8. Pour in your boiled beans
  9. Cover and wait for five minutes and continue stirring
  10. Put in a tablespoon of salt and maggi for taste
  11. Your stew should be ready in 10 minutes
