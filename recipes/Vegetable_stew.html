Vegetable Stew Recipe

Vegetable Stew Stew is one of the nutritional dishes of Ghanaians. It is very healthy and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Smoked Fish
  • Akatewa
  • Meat
  • Spices

Steps In preparing

  1. Chop and wash your Vegetable
  2. Pour your Vegetable oil into your pan
  3. Fry the oil for a while and add your chopped onions
  4. Put in your rose mary spices for aroma and continue to stir
  5. Pour in your blended pepper,onions and tomatoes and continue to stir
  6. Sprinkle your spices and and break the smoked fish to your preferred sizes
  7. Pour in your chopped steamed meat
  8. Pour in your chopped Vegetables
  9. Cover and wait for five minutes and continue stirring
  10. Put in a tablespoon of salt and maggi for taste
  11. Your stew should be ready in 10 minutes
